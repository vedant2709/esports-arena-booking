# E-Sports Arena — Booking & Rewards System

**Turn your gaming café into a smooth, modern booking experience — and keep
players coming back.**

A complete online booking platform built for gaming arenas, PC/console cafés,
racing rigs, and VR lounges. Your customers book and pay online; you manage
everything from one dashboard.

---

## Why arenas use it

- **Fill more slots.** Customers see live availability and book in seconds, 24/7 —
  no phone calls, no double-bookings.
- **Get paid upfront.** Secure online payments mean fewer no-shows and money in
  the bank before they arrive.
- **Bring players back.** A built-in loyalty stamp card rewards regulars with a
  free session — proven to drive repeat visits.
- **Run it from your phone.** A simple admin dashboard shows every booking and
  lets you check players in with one tap.

---

## What your customers get

- See **real-time availability** for every station and pick a slot instantly.
- **Book and pay online** in a clean, guided flow.
- A **digital loyalty card** — earn a stamp each visit, unlock a **free session**.
- Booking confirmations by **email**, plus a "My Bookings" history.

## What you (the owner) get

- A **dashboard** of all bookings — filter, view, and cancel.
- **One-tap check-in** that rewards loyal customers automatically.
- Add a stamp for **walk-in** players in seconds (by phone number).
- **No double-bookings, ever** — the system guarantees one booking per slot.
- Prices and payments handled securely on the server — nothing to fiddle with.

---

## The loyalty program (built in)

> Play 10 sessions, get your next solo session **free**.

- Customers earn a stamp **only when they actually show up and play** — so rewards
  are honest and never gamed.
- The free reward is redeemed online at checkout — pulling customers back into
  your booking page.
- Every stamp is logged, so balances are always clear and disputes are easy to
  settle.
